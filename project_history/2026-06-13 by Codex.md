## Summary

On June 13, 2026, we created and iterated on `jpmap`, an R package for
Japan maps inspired by `usmap`. The package goal became: make Japan maps
with visible Okinawa and Ogasawara insets, support prefecture and municipal
boundaries, provide pkgdown documentation, include realistic example map
workflows, and preserve a CRAN-friendly data strategy based on official MLIT
N03 administrative boundary sources.
